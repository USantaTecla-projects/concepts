What is Concepts?

Santa Tecla Concepts It's the first part of a TFG about building a Web Application based on Angular and Spring Boot, which aims to help teachers and students to improve their learning skills and methodologies.

With Concepts the students can have adapted sets of questions depending on their knowledge level related to some concepts, so in that way the student can know which are his main weak points and the teacher can take note on that information to help the student thanks to this feedback system.

Who can use Concpepts?

Santa Tecla Concepts It's used by teachers and students.

The teachers are responsible for feeding the system with concepts,which can have correct and incorrect definitions, which also can have correct and incorrect justifications, this information is used later to generate exams.

The students are the ones who will take the exams to discover how much the know about a certain topic. At the moment is not implemented, but the idea is that the students get more and more dificult exams meanwhile they start to get better on a certain concept.

Why Concpepts?

Santa Tecla Concepts It's one of the firsts projects that try to improve the learning experience by breaking up with the traditional methodologies where all the students get into a class and all the information is given to them, at the same speed and assuming that everyone is understading that information.

By adapting the information to each individual, Santa Tecla Concepts wants to demostrate that there are other ways to learn.
